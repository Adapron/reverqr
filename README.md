**Instant clipboard sharing between your phone and computer**

ReverQR is a simple web app that lets you quickly transfer text from your phone to your computer using QR codes and peer-to-peer connections.

## Why ReverQR?

- **No apps to install** - Works directly in any web browser
- **No accounts required** - No sign-ups, logins, or data storage
- **Private & secure** - Direct peer-to-peer connection, no data goes through servers
- **Lightning fast** - Instant transfer once connected
- **Works anywhere** - Both devices just need internet access

## How it works

1. **Open** the HTML file on your computer
2. **Scan** the QR code with your phone
3. **Copy** any text on your phone
4. **Tap** "Send Clipboard" 
5. **Text appears** instantly on your computer!
